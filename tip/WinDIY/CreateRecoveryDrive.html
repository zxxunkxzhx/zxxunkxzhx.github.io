Found product serial number

Such as for Surface Laptop model 1950( Laptop 4), we can find it from the device's backside. it's something looks like 035112213857.

Goto https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/surface-recovery-image to download it

I select Surface Laptop4 13in i7/16/512 - Windows 11 Home Version 23H2

Unzip the downloaded into C:\Users\xzhxj\Downloads\SurfaceLaptop4_BMR_12010_2024.513.78

Create the USB recovery drive(better use SSD USB)

  • Creating a recovery drive will erase everything that's stored on your USB drive
  • In the search box on the taskbar, enter recovery drive
  • Make sure to clear the "Back up system files to the recovery drive" check box(we're not create recovery drive for this machine, we are instead for the Laptop4)
  • Select your USB drive, and then select Create; When the recovery drive is ready(there will be at least a folder EFI/ created in the USB), select Finish.
  • COPY the downloaded files into your usb drive, Choose to replace the files in the destination.

    Congratulations, good luck!
