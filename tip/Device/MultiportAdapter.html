Required

  • Power delivery: > 99W
  • MicroSD: if you have a SD card, you can take out the MicroSD to read; so if the Multiport Adapter supports MicroSD, it's enough.
  • USB-A + USB-C > 3
  • HDMI: HDMI is commonly used in meetings or classrooms to project content from a computer or other device onto a large screen; 4K resolution required

Optional

  • RJ45: This kind of cable is very popular, yet normally you already have a RJ45->USB-A Adapter(or you can connect through Wifi)
  • DisplayPort: HDMI is more popular, when Dp is in need, we can have a HDMI->Dp Adapter
